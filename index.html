Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our appliance repair services

Do you offer same-day repair services?

+

Yes! We offer same-day repair services for most appliances when you call before 2 PM. Emergency services are also available on weekends and holidays.

What brands do you service?

+

We service all major appliance brands including Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, LG, KitchenAid, Maytag, Frigidaire, Bosch, and many more. Our technicians are trained on all popular models.

Do you provide warranties on repairs?

+

Yes, all our repairs come with a 90-day warranty on parts and labor. If the same issue occurs within the warranty period, we'll fix it at no additional charge.

What are your service charges?

+

We charge a $75 diagnostic fee which is waived if you proceed with the repair. Our repair rates are competitive and we provide upfront pricing before starting any work.

Do you offer maintenance services?

+

Absolutely! We offer preventive maintenance services to keep your appliances running efficiently and extend their lifespan. Regular maintenance can prevent costly repairs.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

+

Yes, all our technicians are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance to protect your home and property during service calls.
