Extend Your Dishwasher's Life

Regular maintenance can prevent many common dishwasher problems and extend your appliance's lifespan. Our technicians provide these maintenance recommendations:

Clean Filters Regularly

Remove and clean dishwasher filters monthly to prevent clogs and improve cleaning performance

Check Spray Arms

Inspect and clean spray arm holes to ensure proper water circulation during wash cycles

Use Proper Detergent

Use high-quality dishwasher detergent and rinse aid for optimal cleaning and spot prevention

Load Correctly

Proper loading ensures dishes get clean and prevents damage to spray arms and racks
